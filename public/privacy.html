Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to MoodFilm. Your privacy matters. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

MoodFilm does not require registration. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics through standard hosting services.
  • Search Queries: Your mood selections and search text are sent to Google Gemini API to generate recommendations. We do not store these on our servers.
  • Favorites: Movies you save are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI: Processes your queries. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • TMDb: Provides movie data and images. See TMDb's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Loads typography. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

MoodFilm uses browser localStorage only to save your favorite movies on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers. Clear it anytime via your browser settings.

4. Advertising

MoodFilm may display Google AdSense advertisements. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

MoodFilm is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
