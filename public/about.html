About MoodFilm

The story behind your AI movie companion

What is MoodFilm?

MoodFilm is a free AI-powered movie recommendation platform that finds the perfect film based on how you feel right now. Pick your mood or describe what you want to watch — our AI delivers instant personalized suggestions with real movie posters, ratings, and details.

How It Works

MoodFilm combines two powerful technologies working together:

  • Google Gemini AI — understands your mood and generates personalized movie suggestions with reasoning about why each film fits.
  • TMDb (The Movie Database) — provides official posters, ratings, release dates, genres, and full movie metadata.

Features

  • 🎭 8 mood categories — Happy, Sad, Romantic, Thriller, Sci-Fi, Action, Horror, Animation
  • 🔍 Smart AI search — describe anything ("emotional sci-fi", "movies like Interstellar")
  • 🎬 Real movie posters and TMDb ratings on every card
  • ❤️ Save favorites that persist across sessions
  • 🔥 Weekly trending movies section
  • 📱 Fully responsive on all devices
  • ♾️ Unlimited suggestions — AI never repeats itself
  • 💰 100% free — no account, no payment, no limits

Privacy

MoodFilm does not require an account. Your favorites are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or store personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
